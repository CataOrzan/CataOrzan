Project description

The project consists of a design for a small power transformer. The transformer is designed to have two secondary windings, with voltages of 11.5 V and 7.2 V, and currents of 6.1 A and 6.05 A, respectively. The primary winding is designed for a voltage of 230 V and a frequency of 50 Hz. The document includes calculations for the number of turns of wire in each winding, the diameter of the wire, and the type of iron core.
